How it works

From spark to launch in three steps.

No pitch decks, no cold emails. Post what you're building and let the right people come to you.

01

Share your idea

Post a concept, prototype, or launched product with media, tags, and what you're looking for.

02

Find your people

Match with investors, collaborators, and mentors who care about your space — and start the conversation.

03

Build & launch

Schedule calls, track interest, and rally support as your idea grows from a post into a real venture.

Discover

A feed that surfaces the right ideas.

Browse by category, stage, or media type. Save what inspires you and signal interest with a single tap.

  • Smart filters for skills, stage & investment
  • Grid & list views, with saves and stars
  • Trending tags and people you should meet
Create

Publish a polished idea in minutes.

A focused composer with rich text, media uploads, stage and category, and exactly who you're looking for.

  • Photos, video & gallery in one place
  • Tag what you need: investor, collaborator, feedback
  • Save drafts, control visibility, publish when ready
Connect

Real conversations, not cold intros.

Message matches, react to ideas, and schedule calls without leaving the app. Every connection starts with shared intent.

  • Direct messaging with voice notes & reactions
  • Built-in call scheduling across time zones
  • Safety-first: report, block & private accounts
